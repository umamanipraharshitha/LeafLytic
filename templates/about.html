About Us

LeafLytic is a platform designed to revolutionize plant care through advanced technology. Our mission is to empower farmers, gardeners, and researchers by providing quick and accurate plant disease detection. With a simple upload or capture of a leaf image, LeafLytic identifies diseases and offers actionable solutions to ensure healthier plants and improved yields.
